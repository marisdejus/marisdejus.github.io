# Māris Dejus

Māris Dejus is a new media artist, musician, and creative technologist based in Rīga, Latvia, originally from Liepāja.

He makes perceptual instruments — interactive browser-based tools, audiovisual works, and spatial audio experiences that change how people hear and see.

## Practice

His work spans music production, sound design, spatial audio, motion graphics, video art, and creative coding. He has a background in VR and immersive art production, and has exhibited at RIXC and participated in iWeek. He has composed music for theatre productions at Dailes and Liepāja theatres.

He holds an MA in Media Art and Audiovisual Production (LiepU + RISEBA) and a BA in New Media Art, with research focused on immersive virtual environments and 3D audio.

He publishes music under the alias Demaui with over 20 releases on Bandcamp.

## Available for

Commissions, collaborations, and projects involving spatial audio, audiovisual production, creative coding, and immersive or interactive work.
